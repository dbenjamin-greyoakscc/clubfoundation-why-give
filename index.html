FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is The Club Foundation a 501(c)(3)?

Yes. The Club Foundation is the charitable subsidiary of the Club Management Association of America (CMAA) and operates as a 501(c)(3) focused solely on the club industry.

How are scholarships and grants allocated?

Programs are evaluated through an allocation process and volunteer committees, including CF board members, CMAA staff, and members.

Can I give to an endowed fund like the Emery Family Fund?

Yes. The Emery Family Fund is open to contributions from anyone; proceeds support the Foundation where most needed.

Are gifts tax‑deductible?

Gifts to The Club Foundation are generally tax‑deductible in the U.S.; please consult your tax advisor for personal guidance.
